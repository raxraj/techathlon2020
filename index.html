About the Event

Techathlon, an event organized by IPEC, collaborates the intellects of programmers, designers, application developers and other tech-geeks for the intensive development of a software. It aims to provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People with technical backgrounds come together, form a team around an idea and collaboratively try to code a unique solution. These ideas can generally be in the form of websites, applications, projects or posters.
